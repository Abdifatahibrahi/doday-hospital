Our Services

We offer a wide range of medical services to meet all your healthcare needs. Our state-of-the-art facilities and expert staff ensure you receive the best care possible.

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart health services including diagnostics, treatment, and preventive care.

Pediatrics

Specialized care for children from newborns to adolescents with a focus on preventive health.

Neurology

Expert diagnosis and treatment for disorders of the nervous system and brain.

Orthopedics

Treatment for musculoskeletal injuries and conditions, including joint replacement surgery.

Dental Care

Complete dental services for all ages, from routine cleanings to advanced procedures.

Ophthalmology

Comprehensive eye care services including vision correction and treatment of eye diseases.

Doday Medical Center

Doday Medical Center Hospital has been serving the community for over 10 years. We are committed to providing exceptional healthcare services with a patient-centered approach.

Our team of highly skilled medical professionals utilizes the latest technology and evidence-based practices to ensure the best outcomes for our patients.

We believe in treating the whole person, not just the illness, and we strive to create a healing environment that promotes physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being.
